About Us

Olivier IT Services is a French-Hungarian company, based in Paris and Budapest, specialized in SAP UI5, SAP Fiori, SAP Gateway and SCP Consulting & Development. Olivier IT Services has been created in 2015 and served already more than 20 customers. Our ambition is to become one of the references on SAP Fiori and SCP

  • Strengths

    • Implementation of full SAP Fiori Projects: Installation, Configuration, Extensions and Custom Development.
    • Creation and simplification of SAP User Interface Projects (UI) by following the Tools and Methodology of SAP Fiori, S/4HANA and SCRUM.
      For simple Web interfaces, SAP Cloud Portal or Mobile Devices (iOS and Android).

  • Organization

    • The Consulting is locally delivered by our French Team based in France.
    • The Development is remotely delivered by our Near Shore Team based in Budapest.
    • Our Vision of Near Shore Development is to do the developmentmainly remotely with trained and expert international Team in English.
    • The local Coordination is done at the customer site in French, including Requirements, Blueprint, Coordination Meetings, Testing Phase and Acceptance Phase

Our Services



SAP Fiori & UX Strategy

  • Workshops / high level onsite presentations (SAP S/4HANA, SCP, SAP UI5, SAP Fiori)
  • Customer landscape analysis and recommendations
  • Auditing and strategy
  • SAP UI5/Fiori/Gateway/SCP Trainings
  • Time & material expert remote developers

SAP Fiori Installation, Configuration and Support

  • Installation and configuration of SAP Fiori Launchpad and SAP Fiori Transactional apps
  • Extension of standard SAP Fiori application (official method to modify SAP apps)
  • Configuration of SAP Smart Business Apps and SAP KPI Modeler
  • Support and Maintenance of apps in Production
  • Migration from SCP NEO to Cloud Foundry

SAP UI5 Development

  • Full SAP UI5/OData Project: From management (Scrum) to delivery usually with SCP (SAP Web IDE / BAS) and Support
  • Development of CDS Views and Fiori Elements, Customized UI5 apps for desktop
  • Development of Mobile apps with HAT and MDK (SAP Mobile Services) for offline and specific Mobile apps
  • Development of SAP Fiori-like apps following the Best Practices
  • Development of Custom Tiles and FLP Plug-in based on SAP Gateway or SCP
