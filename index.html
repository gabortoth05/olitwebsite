About Us

Olivier IT Services is a French-Hungarian company, based at Paris and Budapest, specialized in SAP UI5, SAP Fiori, SAP Gateway and SCP Consulting & Development. Olivier IT Services has been created in June 2015 and served already more than 20 customers. Our ambition is to become the reference on SAP Fiori and SCP

  • 1

    Strengths

    Implementation of full SAP Fiori Projects: Installation, Configuration, Standard Modifications and Custom Development. Creation and simplification of SAP User Interface Projects (UI) by following the Tools and Methodology of SAP Fiori, S/4HANA and SCRUM. The Target can be simple Web interfaces, SAP Cloud Portal or Mobile Devices (iOS and Android). SAP UX Consulting and Expertise on demand

  • 2

    Organization

    The Consulting is locally delivered by our French Team based in France. The Development is remotely delivered by our Near Shore Team based in Budapest. Our Vision of Near Shore Development is to do the development only remotely with trained and expert people, but with a local coordination at the customer site and in French: Requirements, Blueprint, Coordination Meetings, Testing Phase and Acceptance Phase
